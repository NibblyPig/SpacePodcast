# External Structures

## Orientation

- Forward — beacon end
- Aft — opposite
- Port / Starboard — lateral
- Dorsal / Ventral — above / below ring plane
- Sunward / Darkward — relative to star

---

## Solar Arrays

Mounted on:

- Dorsal Truss Spine
- Ventral Truss Spine

Articulated panels track star.

---

## Radiators

Mounted on:

- darkward trusses
- separate from solar arrays

---

## Beacon

Located at forward end:

- Transmission Beacon Assembly
- High-gain antenna structures

---

## Docking

Located on spine:

- Primary Docking Arms (Port / Starboard)
- Cargo Dock Nodes

---

## Trusses

- Dorsal Truss Spine
- Ventral Truss Spine
- Forward / Aft Extensions
