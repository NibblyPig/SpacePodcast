# Utility Systems

## Purpose

Provides underlying support infrastructure that enables all station
operations.

## Typical Components

-   service conduits
-   access tunnels
-   control nodes
-   monitoring systems
-   redundant routing systems

## Environmental Characteristics

-   non-public areas
-   variable conditions depending on proximity to systems

## Notes

-   spans entire station
-   not tied to specific functional areas
-   supports power, data, and environmental systems
