# Bulk Storage

## Purpose

Stores large quantities of materials, supplies, or raw resources.

## Typical Components

-   large containment units
-   storage bays
-   access corridors
-   handling systems
-   inventory tracking systems

## Environmental Characteristics

-   variable depending on stored material
-   may include sealed or controlled environments

## Notes

-   supports logistics and distribution systems
-   often adjacent to freight handling areas
