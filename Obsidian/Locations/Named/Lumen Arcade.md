# Lumen Arcade

## Type
Commercial / Social Corridor

## Location
Theta Ring — Quarter 1 (adjacent to Orpheus Transit Hub)

## Overview

Lumen Arcade is a broad internal corridor lined with small kiosks, vending points, and informal seating areas.

It functions as a transitional social space rather than a destination, with people passing through rather than remaining for long periods.

---

## Characteristics

- soft ambient lighting with colour-shift panels
- mixed-use kiosks (food, repair, personal items)
- recessed seating bays along the corridor edges
- low-level background audio from adjacent units

---

## Human Activity Indicators

- kiosks opening progressively through early cycle
- seating areas briefly occupied, then cleared
- intermittent clustering near food dispensers
- occasional queue formation that resolves quickly

---

## Persistent Details

- one kiosk frequently remains closed despite being stocked
- display lighting in the central bay occasionally misaligns colour timing
- a seating cluster near the Orpheus-facing entrance is consistently used more than others

---

## Notes

- useful for light human presence without direct interaction
- supports references to food, objects, and small routines
- good transition space between transit and habitation
