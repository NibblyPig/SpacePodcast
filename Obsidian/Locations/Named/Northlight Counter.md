# Northlight Counter

Location: Delta Ring — Civic Band

Northlight Counter is a small food and supply stand set back from the main corridor, with a narrow serving window and a limited rotating selection.

The offering changes without a fixed schedule, with certain items appearing for a few cycles and then not returning for some time.

The front counter sees short bursts of activity rather than steady use, with quiet periods between them.

Items on the display shelf are sometimes left untouched across multiple cycles, while others are taken quickly as soon as they appear.

A narrow ledge along the wall is used for brief stops, though most movement continues back into the corridor.

The lighting is brighter than the surrounding passage, making the counter visible from a distance despite its position.
