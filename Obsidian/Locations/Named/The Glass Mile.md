# The Glass Mile

Location: Outer Ring — Civic Band

The Glass Mile is a long, uninterrupted observation corridor running along the outer hull, fitted with continuous transparent panels.

It offers a wide-angle view of the station’s exterior structures and the surrounding orbital environment.

The corridor is often quieter than expected given its position, with usage patterns that fluctuate without a clear cycle-based trend.

Sections of the floor show faint wear where people tend to stop and stand, particularly near slight imperfections in the glass that alter the view at certain angles.

One panel near the midpoint produces a mild distortion effect when viewed from specific positions, which has become a place where movement slows briefly.

Seating is minimal, encouraging short pauses rather than extended stays.
