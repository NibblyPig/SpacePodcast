# Lantern Court

Location: Delta Ring — Civic Band

Lantern Court is a recessed open space branching off a main corridor, marked by suspended light fixtures that hang lower than the surrounding ceiling.

The lighting shifts slowly in tone across the cycle, creating a different atmosphere depending on the time.

The space alternates between periods of stillness and brief changes in condition without a clear pattern.

A circular seating arrangement at the centre shows more surface wear than the outer benches.

The court is visible from the corridor but not immediately obvious unless approached directly.
