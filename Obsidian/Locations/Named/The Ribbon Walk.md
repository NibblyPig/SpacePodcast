# The Ribbon Walk

Location: Inner Ring — Habitation Band

The Ribbon Walk is a gently curving corridor that transitions between two habitation levels without a clear break between them.

The incline is gradual enough that the change in elevation is not immediately obvious when moving through it.

The inner edge of the curve shows consistent wear along a narrow path, while the outer edge remains comparatively untouched.

There is a point near the midpoint where movement slows slightly before continuing, though nothing marks it explicitly.

Lighting follows the curve in segmented bands, with slight timing differences between sections that create a soft shifting effect.
