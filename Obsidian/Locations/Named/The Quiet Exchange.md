# The Quiet Exchange

Location: Epsilon Ring — Civic Band

The Quiet Exchange is a small, low-profile cluster of kiosks operating without prominent signage.

It provides item exchange and small services that do not appear on standard station directories.

Units cycle between active and inactive states without a clear pattern, with some remaining closed for multiple cycles before returning.

Objects are sometimes present on counter surfaces, remaining in place across multiple observations without change.

The layout discourages lingering, with narrow spacing and minimal seating.

Lighting is slightly dimmer than surrounding corridors, giving the area a contained, self-contained feel.
