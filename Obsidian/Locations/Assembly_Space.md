# Assembly Space

## Purpose

Provides large-scale gathering areas for organised or informal group
activity.

## Typical Components

-   open halls
-   seating arrangements
-   staging areas
-   audio systems
-   access points

## Environmental Characteristics

-   large open volumes
-   controlled acoustics
-   adjustable lighting

## Notes

-   primarily located in civic and recreation rings
-   supports events, briefings, and general assembly
