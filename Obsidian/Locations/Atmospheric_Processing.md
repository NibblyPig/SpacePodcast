# Atmospheric Processing

## Purpose

Maintains gas composition, pressure balance, and air quality across
controlled environments.

## Typical Components

-   gas mixing systems
-   filtration units
-   pressure regulators
-   circulation systems
-   monitoring sensors

## Environmental Characteristics

-   tightly controlled
-   redundant systems
-   continuous operation

## Notes

-   integrated with environmental control and hydroponics systems
-   critical for life support
