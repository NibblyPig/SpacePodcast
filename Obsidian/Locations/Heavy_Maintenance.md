# Heavy Maintenance

## Purpose

Supports large-scale repair, overhaul, and servicing of major systems
and machinery.

## Typical Components

-   large repair bays
-   lifting systems
-   heavy tool arrays
-   diagnostic systems
-   safety barriers

## Environmental Characteristics

-   reinforced structural areas
-   variable temperature and noise levels

## Notes

-   primarily located in industrial rings
-   supports fabrication and machine systems
