# Isolation Ward

## Purpose

Provides controlled separation for containment, observation, or
quarantine.

## Typical Components

-   sealed rooms
-   airlock access points
-   independent life-support systems
-   monitoring systems
-   restricted access controls

## Environmental Characteristics

-   fully controlled atmosphere
-   isolated from surrounding systems

## Notes

-   used for medical or research containment
-   operates independently from general environments
