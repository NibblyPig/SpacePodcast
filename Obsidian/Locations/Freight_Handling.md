# Freight Handling

## Purpose

Manages loading, unloading, and routing of bulk goods and materials.

## Typical Components

-   loading bays
-   conveyor systems
-   docking interfaces
-   sorting areas
-   control stations

## Environmental Characteristics

-   high activity areas
-   reinforced flooring and structures

## Notes

-   closely linked with storage and distribution
-   prioritises throughput and efficiency
