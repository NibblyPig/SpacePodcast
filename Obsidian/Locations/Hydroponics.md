# Hydroponics

## Purpose

Supports plant growth for food production and atmospheric balancing.

## Typical Components

-   growth trays
-   nutrient delivery systems
-   water circulation loops
-   lighting arrays
-   environmental sensors

## Environmental Characteristics

-   controlled humidity
-   controlled temperature
-   artificial light cycles
-   sealed subsections possible

## Notes

-   often subdivided into multiple zones
-   systems operate continuously
