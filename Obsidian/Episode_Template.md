# Episode Template

## Opening
Brief greeting.
Short transition into operations.

## Core Flow
Loose mixture of:
- local operational updates
- transit notes
- environmental conditions
- social / behavioural reminders
- occasional observational suggestions

## Human Layer
Include:
- reminders (eat, rest, move carefully)
- suggestions (views, quiet spaces)

## Threads
Subtle ongoing elements:
- maintenance
- growth systems
- minor irregularities

## Ending
Signature sign-off:
"Cycle [X] is now concluding. Rest cycles will begin shortly, and all systems will continue to be maintained. This, is System Core. Goodnight."
