# Central Spine

## Description

Primary structural and transit axis of the station.

Non-rotating, zero-gravity environment.

---

## Functions

- transit backbone
- utility routing
- structural support
- docking access

---

## Transit

- automated pods on fixed rails
- multiple parallel lines:
  - passenger (2)
  - freight (1)
  - service (1)
- uninterrupted flow

---

## Spine Nodes

Each ring connects via a Spine Node.

Contains:
- routing systems
- branching tracks
- access to transit couplings

---

## External Interfaces

Mounted on spine:
- docking arms
- truss connections
- transit coupling structures
