# Zeta Ring — Industrial

## Overview

Zeta Ring is the primary industrial and heavy operations district of Elysium.

It supports fabrication, large-scale processing, and mechanical operations. It is the most structurally reinforced and operationally intensive ring.

---

## Core Characteristics

- large ring
- moderate gravity (~0.7–0.9g outer band)
- moderate spoke count (8)
- high mechanical and energy load

---

## Structure

- outer band: fabrication and heavy machinery
- mid band: processing and assembly systems
- inner band: maintenance and utility infrastructure

---

## Ring-Wide Systems

The following systems exist across the entire ring:

- Administration
- Security
- Fire Suppression
- Power Distribution
- Environmental Control
- Transit Control
- Communications
