# Gamma Ring — Research

## Overview

Gamma Ring is the primary research and analytical district of Elysium.

It supports scientific study, controlled experimentation, and specialised analysis. Many areas operate under restricted or isolated conditions.

---

## Core Characteristics

- medium-large ring
- moderate gravity (~0.6–0.8g outer band)
- moderate spoke count (8)
- high density of specialised environments

---

## Structure

- outer band: general research and observation
- mid band: controlled experimentation and diagnostics
- inner band: processing, isolation, and support systems

---

## Ring-Wide Systems

The following systems exist across the entire ring:

- Administration
- Security
- Fire Suppression
- Power Distribution
- Environmental Control
- Transit Control
- Communications
