# Kappa Ring — Storage / Logistics

## Overview

Kappa Ring is the primary storage and logistics district of Elysium.

It supports bulk storage, distribution, and freight handling. It is structured for efficient movement of materials between rings and external docking systems.

---

## Core Characteristics

- large ring
- moderate gravity (~0.7–0.9g outer band)
- moderate spoke count (8)
- high freight throughput

---

## Structure

- outer band: bulk storage and large-volume containment
- mid band: distribution and sorting systems
- inner band: freight handling and transfer systems

---

## Ring-Wide Systems

The following systems exist across the entire ring:

- Administration
- Security
- Fire Suppression
- Power Distribution
- Environmental Control
- Transit Control
- Communications
