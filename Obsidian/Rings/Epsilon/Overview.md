# Epsilon Ring — Recreation / Civic

## Overview

Epsilon Ring is the primary civic and recreational district of Elysium.

It supports assembly, public gathering, education, and non-essential activity spaces. It is less dense than habitation rings and contains larger open-volume environments.

---

## Core Characteristics

- medium-large ring
- slightly reduced gravity (~0.6–0.8g outer band)
- moderate spoke count (10)
- high variability in internal space usage

---

## Structure

- outer band: recreation and large open environments
- mid band: civic and public functions
- inner band: services and support systems

---

## Ring-Wide Systems

The following systems exist across the entire ring:

- Administration
- Security
- Fire Suppression
- Power Distribution
- Environmental Control
- Transit Control
- Communications
