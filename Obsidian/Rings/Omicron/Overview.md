# Omicron Ring — Medical

## Overview

Omicron Ring is the primary medical and care district of Elysium.

It supports treatment, recovery, diagnostics, and controlled isolation environments. It is more tightly regulated than other rings and contains a higher proportion of controlled-access areas.

---

## Core Characteristics

- medium ring
- moderate gravity (~0.7–0.9g outer band)
- moderate spoke count (8)
- controlled access zones throughout

---

## Structure

- outer band: recovery and longer-term care
- mid band: treatment and diagnostics
- inner band: processing, isolation, and support systems

---

## Ring-Wide Systems

The following systems exist across the entire ring:

- Administration
- Security
- Fire Suppression
- Power Distribution
- Environmental Control
- Transit Control
- Communications
