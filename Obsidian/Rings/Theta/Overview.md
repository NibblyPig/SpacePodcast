# Theta Ring — Habitation Primary

## Overview

Theta Ring is the primary habitation district of Elysium.

It contains the highest density of residential, civic, and day-to-day operational environments.

---

## Core Characteristics

- largest ring
- near-standard gravity (~1g outer band)
- high spoke count (12)
- high transit volume

---

## Structure

- outer band: habitation and daily activity
- mid band: civic and public systems
- inner band: services and distribution

---

## Ring-Wide Systems

The following systems exist across the entire ring and are not tied to a specific quarter:

- Administration
- Security
- Fire Suppression
- Power Distribution
- Environmental Control
- Transit Control
- Communications
