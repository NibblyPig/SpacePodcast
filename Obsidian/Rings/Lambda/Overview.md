# Lambda Ring — Habitation Secondary

## Overview

Lambda Ring provides secondary habitation capacity and supports overflow from Theta Ring.

It contains residential, public service, and general-purpose environments, with slightly lower density and less civic concentration than Theta Ring.

---

## Core Characteristics

- large ring
- near-standard gravity (~1g outer band)
- moderate spoke count (10)
- lower transit intensity than Theta Ring

---

## Structure

- outer band: habitation and daily living
- mid band: public services and mixed-use
- inner band: services and infrastructure

---

## Ring-Wide Systems

The following systems exist across the entire ring:

- Administration
- Security
- Fire Suppression
- Power Distribution
- Environmental Control
- Transit Control
- Communications
