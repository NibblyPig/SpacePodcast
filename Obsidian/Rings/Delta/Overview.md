# Delta Ring — Hydroponics / Environmental Production

## Overview

Delta Ring is the primary environmental production district of Elysium.

It supports food cultivation, atmospheric processing, and biological cycling systems. The ring is heavily partitioned into controlled environments and operates under tightly regulated conditions.

---

## Core Characteristics

- large ring
- moderate gravity (~0.6–0.8g outer band)
- high spoke count (10)
- high volume of fluid, gas, and biological systems

---

## Structure

- outer band: large-scale cultivation environments
- mid band: growth control and processing systems
- inner band: nutrient, water, and atmospheric infrastructure

---

## Ring-Wide Systems

The following systems exist across the entire ring:

- Administration
- Security
- Fire Suppression
- Power Distribution
- Environmental Control
- Transit Control
- Communications
