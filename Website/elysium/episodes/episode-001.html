The Maintenance Interval

Filed Cycle 001, Day 14
Duration 38 minutes
Classification General Access

Summary

Chief Engineer Voss completes a scheduled inspection of the lower decks — a task that should take no more than two hours. It takes six. The environmental logs for the secondary maintenance corridor show readings that cannot have been produced by any equipment currently installed on the station. The entries have been manually altered. Voss files the inspection as routine.

A measured opening. The station is introduced through one person's precise, methodical attention. What she notices, and what she chooses not to say, establishes the tone of everything that follows.
