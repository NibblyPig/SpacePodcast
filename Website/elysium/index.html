Space Station Elysium

Sleepy operational logs recovered from the station archive.

Elysium is a serialised set of audio logs set aboard a deep-space station at the edge of maintained territory. Each transmission represents a single cycle of mundane ordinary life aboard this distant outpost. Announcements, notices, observations, and updates. Just softly spoken fragments that catalogue nothing much at all.
